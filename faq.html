FAQ

1) Team size?.
1-4 members

2) How can I registerfor the Comptetion?.
Fill this Google Form here

3) Possibility of usage of AW-IDD labels in Safe-Segmentation-Competition (SS-C).
Only the labels related to SS-C can be used.

4) Can multiple teams from same college participate?
Yes

5) Is there any rule for the team to be formed?
No

6) For SS, can we modify source datasets to fit our model?
Yes, make sure that you only use the samples we have allowed.

7) Could I use pseudo label method in SS-C?
You can use any method. Use of any dataset outside permitted samples and labels is not allowed.

8) Procedure to register the team.
We have added an option to enter the team member's name (comma separated) . The team should consist of not more than 4 members.

9) Meaning of affiliation
Its your academic institution or organization.

10) May I use IDD detection datasets to SS-C?
No, you can only use allowed samples (or a sub-set from them) and allowed labels.

11) For SS-C, may I use a part of source data you given? Not use all of it.
Yes, you can use the subset from samples we have allowed.

12) For supervised SS-A, is it ok to train on source and fine-tune on target samples?
Yes, you can do that only for SS-C, make sure that you use only the samples allowed for training from source data.
Note that you need to be better than baselines for SS-C related challenges to be a winner.
Refer to the short summary on SS-C website
