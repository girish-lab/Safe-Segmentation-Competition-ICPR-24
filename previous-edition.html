Second International Workshop On Autonomous Navigation in Unconstrained Environments (AutoNUE 2019)
The Second Edition of AutoNUE workshop, held at ICCV 2019, Seoul, Korea, had over 200 attendees at the event. As part of AutoNUE 2019, new datasets were released:

  • IDD 20K
  • IDD Lite
  • IDD Multimodal

Four challenges were organized in the workshop: Intel sponsored prize money of USD1000 for each challenge to the Winner and Runner-Up.

  • Segmentation
  • Panoptic Segmentation
  • Segmentation on Constrained Devices
  • Localization

The workshop had six invited keynote speakers from diverse backgrounds:

  • Prof. Ayoung Kim
  • Dr.Adrien Gaidon
  • Dr. Kai Yu
  • Dr. Li Erran Li
  • Prof.Raquel Urtasun
  • Prof. Torsten Sattler

First International Workshop On Autonomous Navigation in Unconstrained Environments (AutoNUE 2018)
The First Edition of AutoNUE workshop September 8-September 14, Munich, Germany in Conjunction with ECCV 2018, had over 200 attendees. The highlights of the workshop were three high-quality keynote speakers:

  • Prof. Jitendra Malik
  • Prof. Andreas Geiger
  • Prof. Vladlen Koltun

As part of AutoNUE 2018,new datasets were released: IDD-Detection, IDD-Segmentation. Two challenges were organized in the workshop: Intel sponsored prize money of USD1000 for each challenge to the Winner and Runners-Up.

  • Semantic Segmentation
  • Instance Segmentation
